Milk Steak

Delicous meal for the whole family!!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Steak
  • Jelly Beans

Steps

  1. Pour some milk into a pot and also add a nice hunk of meat
  2. Turn on the stove and wait until the milk starts smoking
  3. Pour some jelly beans in there for flavor
  4. Wait about 5 to 20 minutes and enjoy!
